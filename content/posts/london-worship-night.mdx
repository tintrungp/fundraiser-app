# London Team Worship Night

Last Friday our London missions team gathered for one final night of worship and prayer before we leave in July. It was one of those evenings where the Holy Spirit just showed up in a big way — a room full of people lifting their voices and surrendering the trip to God.

This song has kind of become the anthem for our team going into London. Give it a listen and feel free to be praying these words over us!

## What We're Praying For

As we head into London, here are the things weighing on our hearts:

- **Open doors** for meaningful conversations about faith
- **Boldness** to share the Gospel even when it feels uncomfortable
- **Unity** among our team as we serve together in a new city
- **Provision** — we're still fundraising and God has been so faithful!

Thank you for being part of this. Every prayer and every donation is sending us to London. 🙏
