# Hi 👋

This summer I have the great pleasure of being able to run the Rock 'n' Roll Marathon for
Hope for San Diego as well as going on a Missions Trip to London with Redeemer. It's truly
been a blessing to be able to do these things, and I've seen how each commitment has come 
from an overflow of how God has been working in my life.

With those two exciting things that I'm working toward this summer comes a lot of fundraising
too! As a means of fundraising I'm trying my best to make content (the influencer in me is 
beginning to come out) but also update those who are interested in my journey. Because while
making content and get creative with ways to fundraise is a whole lot of fun, something I value
most is the ways in which I get to see God working. What a great ask, to try and fundraise for
two events in the same summer. Yet, even so, God has been so faithful in providing. I get to 
share about how He's providing for me, but also how He's humbling me and leading me to surrender
and depend more fully on Him. And His means? The people in my life.

I made a YouTube video a few years ago. In it, I wrote "I take a lot of things for granted. I 
don't want people to be one them."
